Rankings

The MBAFCS Business School Paris is glad to be integrated in different ranking categories :

 

The Emolument site has listed the 30 institutions with the best paid graduates, after five to ten years’ experience. This study examined a panel of 5,622 graduates from European schools or universities.

 

The ESLSCA Business School ranks in the top 10 European schools and in the Top 4 French schools, with graduates paid more than 100,000 Euros per year: HEC, Mines, Polytechnic and MBAFCS Business School Paris.

 

The International Trade Monitor (MOCI) ranks MBAFCS Business School Paris:

 

1st for the Executive MBA from MBAFCS Business School Paris in the Top Executive MBA courses

 

1st for the Bachelor’s in Business Administration from the Business School ESLSCA Paris in the Top Undergraduate courses

 

SMBG Eduniversal conducted a ranking in which different MBAFCS Business School Paris programs are present:

 

  • The Trading - Financial Markets MBA ranks 5th in the Finance and Portfolio Management category in the 2017 ranking.

 

  • The Luxury and Fashion Management and Marketing MBA is ranked 5th in the Luxury Management category.

 

  • The Strategic Management and Economic Intelligence MBA is ranked as the 7th best Master’s degree in the world in 2017 in the category “Master’s in Business Intelligence, Knowledge Management and Security.”

 

  • The Economic Strategy and Intelligence MBA is ranked as the 8th best Master’s in the world in the “Master’s in Business Intelligence, Knowledge Management and Security” category

 

  • The Bachelor’s of Business Administration ranks 12th in the “International Management” category.

 

  • The Global MBA is ranked 18th in the category " Full Time MBA "

 

  • The Program Grande Ecole of ' MBAFCS Business School Paris ranks in the top 20 of the Grande Ecole Program post prepa.

 

  • The Grande Ecole Program is the winner of the Educational Trophy.

 

  • The Digital Markting and E-Business MBA Training Course won the Innovation Prize in the 2017 ranking.

 

  • The International Business Law - Management and Finance MBA Training Course won the Innovation Prize in the 2017 ranking.
