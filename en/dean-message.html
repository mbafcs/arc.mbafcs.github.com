Dean message

Alexandra JOSEPH

Welcome to IFAM !

 

Dear students,

 

Since 1982 and the foundation of IFAM Business School, I have tried to develop an education scheme based on the meeting of two strong academic standards : USA and France. IFAM Business School is at the middle point of this exciting challenge : Promoting American management education in the leading European country.

 

Ideally located in the heart of Paris, IFAM Business School benefits of the exceptional business as well as cultural melting pot offered by one of the most dynamic European main city.

 

Our programs, whether undergraduate (BBA) or graduate (MBA), attract students from all over the world. All our students take advantage of the unique international student body (more than 40 nationalities are represented on our campus). By getting to know, understanding and working with each other, our students are very early facing the tremendous challenges of global perspective.

 

IFAM Business School has always intended to develop the future managers of international leading companies. By following them closely, I am always proud of the alumni (more than 2 500) working in so many different fields (banking, advertising, luxury marketing, consulting, pharmaceutical companies,…) and in so many various countries (USA, India, China, Argentina, Morocco, Senegal, Russia, France…).

 

Joining IFAM Business School is the best way for students to become the new managers of a constantly evolving business environment.

 

I encourage you to actively read our website and to visit it often. It will take you on a fascinating online journey through the vibrant and diverse university community that is IFAM - "Where Knowledge Becomes Power."

 

Alexandra JOSEPH
